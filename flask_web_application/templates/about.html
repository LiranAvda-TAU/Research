About Our Project

We aim to offer you tools that will help you be a more efficient researcher!

We invite you to go ahead and try our homologs tool (to find C.elegans\Human homologs for your Human\C.elegans genes), our variants tool and our automated CRIPSR planner

Orthologs Finder Pipeline:

A web interface that allow users to insert human genes and get their C.elegans orthologs.

Automated CRIPSR Planner:

A service that will plan the CRISPR for your gene, given only the gene name, the mutated amino acid site and the current and desired amino acid
